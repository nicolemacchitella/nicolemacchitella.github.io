2025

Money Illusion in Large Language Models: An Exploratory Replication Study

with Milos Ciganovic & Luca Panaccione

Journal of Behavioral Economics for Policy, Vol. 9, Issue 1

We explore whether major Large Language Models show money illusion, replicating scenarios from Shafir, Diamond, and Tversky (1997). Testing six models across twelve economic scenarios, we find that LLMs generally reason in real terms rather than nominal ones, though their answers sometimes align with human biases in more subjective situations.
